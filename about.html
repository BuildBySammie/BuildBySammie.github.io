Hello I am Sammie

I am a Designer, Maker, Tinkerer. My entire life I have loved to create envision and build stuff. As a kid this manifested prodemently in lego, but i quickly learned to use tools to make any thing out of raw materials. Even learning to weld when i was twelve.

Now I love to put my time in personal 3D printing and electronics / Arduino projects. Creating stuff to make my life easier, to learn a new skill or just for the heck of it. Loving to build and wanting to know how everything around me works sparked my interest in design. Resulting in me applying to Industrial Design Engineering at the TU Delft. Here I began to understand design on a deeper level and started to understand what design means to me. To me design is creating / making, going from nothing to something. Design doesn't always need to solve a problem but always needs to be useful. And most importantly design should be made to last a life time or even more.

“Jack of all trades, master of none, though often better than a master of one”
